Northern Lights
Minibus Tour

The most personal aurora experience in Tromsø. With a maximum of 15 guests, your guide has time for everyone. We load into our comfortable minibus and follow the aurora forecast in real time — chasing clear skies across mountains, fjords and, if needed, all the way across the Finnish border. No time limits. No compromises. We stay out until every single person has seen the northern lights.
